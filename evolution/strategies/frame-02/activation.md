# Frame 2 strategy audit — Activation

## Observed in the live Frame 1

- The first useful action sat below a metrics block and asked for six intake dimensions before showing a mission.
- Goal, why, obstacle, proof, timebox, and confidence made the user design the experiment instead of receiving assistance.
- A fresh user could not reach review with only the irreducible job and constraint.

## Proposal

Lead with two text inputs—goal and binding constraint—and one deterministic proof-pattern choice: ask a person, make an artifact, check a constraint, or send a reversible probe. Default the five-minute timebox and defer why/confidence. Keep drafts out of history and metrics.

## Acceptance signal

A fresh browser reaches an editable mission after two text entries and one proof choice; the workspace precedes metrics and history.

## Consensus votes (1–5)

- Assisted experiment compiler: **5**
- Criterion-linked evidence and decision: **4**
- Recoverable local workspace: **4**
