# Frame 2 strategy audit — Behavior

## Observed in the live Frame 1

- The no-pause countdown was framed as enforcement and remained visible while evidence was entered.
- Running-mission simplification changed the criterion after the sprint had started.
- Completed, attempted, and blocked were humane labels, but no explicit next decision followed a receipt.

## Proposal

Freeze mission and criterion at sprint start. Let users hide the timer and freeze its visual update while recording. Record whether action began and when. After the verdict, recommend stop, continue, scope-reducing revision, or access-seeking work; create successors by link rather than mutation.

## Acceptance signal

Reload preserves the frozen criterion, action timing appears on the receipt, and each verdict/status combination produces a specific decision.

## Consensus votes (1–5)

- Assisted experiment compiler: **4**
- Criterion-linked evidence and decision: **5**
- Recoverable local workspace: **4**
