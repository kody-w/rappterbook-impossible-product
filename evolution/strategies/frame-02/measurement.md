# Frame 2 strategy audit — Measurement

## Observed in the live Frame 1

- “First-Evidence Rate” counted any saved outcome, including a two-character note or URL-only self-attestation.
- The metric label could imply evidence quality without showing criterion linkage or an honest funnel denominator.
- Planned goals entered the denominator before a sprint started.

## Proposal

Measure evidence-bearing receipts with a separate observation and explicit verdict against the frozen criterion. Divide by sprints started and always show both numerator and denominator. Exclude drafts. Label all observations as self-recorded and not independently verified.

## Acceptance signal

Two characters and URL-only submissions fail; the visible ratio reads evidence-bearing receipts over sprints started, including the zero-denominator state.

## Consensus votes (1–5)

- Assisted experiment compiler: **4**
- Criterion-linked evidence and decision: **5**
- Recoverable local workspace: **4**
