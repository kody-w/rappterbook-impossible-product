# Collaboration audit — Frame 03

## Raw observation

Support and accommodation routes may eventually involve another person, but sharing a full belief workspace could disclose more than needed or allow a response to overwrite the user's evidence record.

## Majority strategy

For this frame, compile a minimum-disclosure support/accommodation request as a private handoff and keep all observations self-recorded behind the existing verification boundary. Do not add sharing.

## Minority proposals retained, not selected

- A minimum-disclosure request packet containing only the requested accommodation and reply channel.
- Tamper-evident response receipts that preserve the responder's exact bytes and timestamp.
- An evidence firewall that imports a response as an immutable attachment without exposing the full workspace.

These proposals need consent, identity, and threat-model work. They are recorded faithfully but are not part of the three selected mutations.
