# Experience audit — Frame 03

## Raw observation

The Frame 2 review was compact, but the useful object was still described as a mission. Users needed an executable handoff, a single next decision, and immediate orientation to the belief being tested.

## Strategy

Put the assumption-first entry in the first viewport, place the copy-ready artifact in the primary review path, and fold advanced directional editing behind details. On receipt, present one recommended decision with all alternatives available and render a keyboard-readable lineage plus concise local brief.

## Acceptance signal

- First viewport asks for the decision and uncertain belief.
- “Copy & begin” is a real state transition.
- Advanced detail remains available without overwhelming review.
- Focus, reflow, and browser error checks cover every populated panel.
