# Inclusive-design audit — Frame 03

## Raw observation

A compulsory countdown and forced confidence number can punish low energy, disability, waiting, safety constraints, or inaccessible systems. Retaliation-sensitive asks need a non-contact default.

## Strategy

Offer safe routes and capacity-aware pacing: countdown, active-effort cap, or untimed. For ask/send probes, response waiting must pause active effort. Confidence before and after is nullable. Blocked and safe-stop receipts remain dignified evidence without invented percentages.

## Acceptance signal

- Unsafe fixtures never compile outbound contact without explicit opt-in.
- Untimed probes have no synthetic deadline.
- Waiting does not consume active effort.
- Blank confidence round-trips through blocked receipts.
- 320px, 200% text, and keyboard focus remain release gates.
