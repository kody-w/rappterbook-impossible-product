# Science audit — Frame 03

## Raw observation

The frozen criterion was explicit but not directional. “Observed” could mean a probe was delivered, an artifact opened, or the belief-relevant signal appeared. Those states have different epistemic value.

## Strategy

Represent the uncertain assumption as target/unit, claim, observable signal, and decision informed. Freeze a diagnostic matrix before action: what supports, what weakens, and what remains inconclusive. Require an evidence threshold or equivalent branch language that excludes sending, opening, and completion by themselves.

## Acceptance signal

- All three branches are visible and persisted.
- A copy-ready payload or artifact/check skeleton is generated.
- Interpretation remains directional and explicitly non-statistical.
- Weak test, absent signal, rival explanation, and blocked access are separate diagnoses.
